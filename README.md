> **Create beautiful, fluid QR codes right in your browser.**

iLoveQR is a professional-grade QR code generator built with a focus on **design**, **privacy**, and **speed**. Unlike traditional generators that track scans or send data to servers, iLoveQR runs entirely on the client-side. Your data never leaves your device.
